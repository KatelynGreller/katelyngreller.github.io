I'm Katelyn Greller.

I’m a philosophy and UX grad seeking opportunities in NYC. I was drawn to a career in UX when I started thinking about the ethics of persuasive design. I love finding the narrative in design problems be it through user journeys or copywriting. I also blog about tech here. I’m obsessed with Post-It notes, pencils and pens.



MHEC Information Architecture

 When the Maryland Higher Education Commission approached our class to redesign their IA, my group saw an opportunity to reposition MHEC as the state’s premiere higher education access resource. I took a leading role in developing personas, scenarios, and brand strategy that reflect the key themes of access, success, and innovation.

BOPA App Design

 The Baltimore Office of Promotion & the Arts was looking to close the gap between public art and public citizens with a mobile app that lets residents suggest murals in their own communities. My group prototyped our proposed app from low to high fidelity with an emphasis on industry best practice.


HCI Case Study

From researching challenges in the area of voice UI I discovered that digital assistants are underutilized for online shopping. Using concepts from my coursework on influence, persuasion and perception, I proposed a solution that uses light and sound to encourage voice purchases.
