HCI Case Study

For my coursework in HCI I designed a case study based on nudging behavior in a defined user population. I started by researching challenges in the area of voice UI. I discovered that, despite their popularity, Alexa and Google Home are underutilized when it comes to online shopping. Using concepts from our work on influence, persuasion and perception, I proposed a solution that uses light and sound to encourage voice purchases.
